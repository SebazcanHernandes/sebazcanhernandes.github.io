About Sebastian:

   Sebastian's self-titled debut album features 9 new tracks written by him as well as his 2 previous iconic singles. This new album introduces an expansion on pop music, alternative rock, jazz, and other formats, as well as Sebastian's newfound capabilities as a vocalist, producer, and composer.
