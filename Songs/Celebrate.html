Celebrate

Song Bio:

   Even with a busy life, just remember that there are things worth celebrating, whether it's memorable or insignificant.

Background:

   This track is the closest to a live recording to any official song by Sebastian with him strumming along as he sung. "Celebrate" is meant to close the track on a reflective note, hoping for the listener to remain optimistic and appreciate life for what it is. This song is also a form of experimental music with guitars found playing in the background that contain added effects for a serene atmosphere to take shape. Sebastian found this to be his favorite track off the EP.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: December 1, 2024
  • First Released In: A Breeze of Hernandez
  • Genre/s: Holiday, Acoustic pop, Ambient, Experimental pop
