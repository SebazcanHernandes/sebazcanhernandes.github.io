(As Long As) You're Still Here

Song Bio:

   The first holiday-related song by Sebastian, it brings about the jingle of Christmas we're all familiar with. Though not too special, the song is a part of his songwriting journey at the time.

Background:

   Writen back at late 2022, the song was first performed for a Christmas party in his class. It later ended up getting studio treatment for the Xmas single of 2023. Originally, Sub wanted the song to be an A-side before inside critics preferred "The Glowing Tree" to be the A-side instead.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: November 1, 2023
  • First Released In: "You're Still Here" b/w "(As Long As) You're Still Here" (2023)
  • Genre/s: Christmas, Pop
