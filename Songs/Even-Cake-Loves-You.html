Even Cake Loves You

Song Bio:

   This is not your usual birthday song. With or without cake, be happy that you made it.

Background:

   Taking a few steps back to explore his roots once more, Sebastian wrote "Even Cake Loves You" with a standard blues progression (I-IV-V), albeit used differently to add a cozy interpretation. The song is dedicated to CerberVT, much like the rest of the EP where this song originates from. References include the mention of cuteness and the dedication of sending love.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: November 1, 2025
  • First Released In: For Wan Dear Friend (2025)
  • Genre/s: Indie rock, Rockabilly
