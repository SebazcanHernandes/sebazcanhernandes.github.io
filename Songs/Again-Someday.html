Again Someday

Song Bio:

   Relationships are complicated, so to make things simple, this song is exactly that. Complicated still, but easier to comprehend.

Background:

   One of Sebastian's earliest works dating back in mid-2021, the main piano riff was written as an exploration to sadder melodies. The song was originally never meant to be a love song, but its development led to lyrics of breakup denial.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: January 29, 2024
  • First Released In: Sebastian
  • Genre/s: Pop, Ballad
