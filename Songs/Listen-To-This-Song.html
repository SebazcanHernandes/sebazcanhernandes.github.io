Listen To This Song

Song Bio:

   An odd opener to an odd debut album, "Listen To This Song" encapsulates what is to come from the listening experience and you may as well enjoy it. The song offers the perspective of enjoying life to the fullest no matter what.

Background:

   Strangely enough, this was Sub's actual first written song originating back in mid-2021 through the opening piano riff. The chorus originates from a session of learning guitar where he and a sibling played around with lyrics. The song was finalized around late 2023 as the song we know today.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: January 29, 2024
  • First Released In: Sebastian
  • Genre/s: Rock and roll, Pop
