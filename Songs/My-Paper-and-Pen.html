My Paper and Pen

Song Bio:

   Ever wondered what it meant to be creative? It's all about company, with a paper and pen. Work your way and learn as Sub swings your body to the melody for what it means to be creative.

Background:

   Not wanting to shy away from other genres, Sebastian continued to pursue jazz styles, and this particular track was secured as the successor to "The Good Times." What makes the track unique is the use of just bass and percussion as a backing track, focused entirely on building the base of a song while letting the vocals take over to do the job.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: June 15, 2024
  • First Released In: Memories From My Own Dreams
  • Genre/s: Rhythm and blues, Jazz
