Hello, Teacher

Song Bio:

   The first song by Sub to be completed.

   A simple guitar song, "Hello, Teacher" embraces simple music. Nothing much to say here other than its significance in Sub's songwriting journey.

Background:

   While still in the early stages of learning guitar, Sub discovered the simple chord progression which only made its way into a song in mid-2022. He only had his guitar back then, so the master version of the song only requires a guitar and vocals. The song was written and first performed for a Teachers' Day party in 2022.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: July 25, 2023
  • First Released In: "Where'd You Go?" b/w "Hello, Teacher" (2023)
  • Genre/s: Folk
