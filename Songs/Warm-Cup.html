Warm Cup

Song Bio:

   Here's a toast to the late year holiday season! And for the Philippines, it happens to be a mess of cold rain and strong sun rays. Sub's asking to stay warm through the harsh breeze. Typical Sebastian.

Background:

   For this track, Sub went back to return to his more musical roots, particularly 50's rock and roll. Feeling more bold and genuine, he chose to write a song complaining about the usual business of the holiday season. The main focus of the song is about the cold winds, with small mentions of overplayed songs and presents.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: December 1, 2024
  • First Released In: A Breeze of Hernandez
  • Genre/s: Holiday, Rockabilly, Indie rock
