Where'd You Go?

Song Bio:

   A stronger rock and roll song by Sub, this delves more into the use of melody. Though not as enjoyed as "Peace and Quiet", it remains a note of development of the young singer-songwriter.

Background:

   Written in early 2023, As an expansion to his exploration of rock and roll, Sub wanted to try something stronger. Making use of distortion, riffs, and backing vocals, this song rose the standards of the songwriter, especially with later work such as song covers and other originals.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: July 25, 2023
  • First Released In: "Where'd You Go?" b/w "Hello, Teacher" (2023)
  • Genre/s: Rock and Roll
