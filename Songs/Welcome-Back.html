Welcome Back (We Missed You)

Song Bio:

   How was your trip? There are dawgs waiting for you at your porch. We welcome you back home!

Background:

   The riff that led to the creation of this song was discovered after Sebastian played around with sliding. A recent influence around this time was Jeff Buckley, which provided him the use of a strange chord progression that managed to work itself out. The song is dedicated to CerberVT, much like the rest of the EP where this song originates from. References include minawan, wan wan, and Cerber's trip to Japan.

  • First Released In: For Wan Dear Friend (2025)
