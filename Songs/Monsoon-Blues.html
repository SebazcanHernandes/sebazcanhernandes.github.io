Monsoon Blues

Song Bio:

   "WHY IS IT ALWAYS RAINING?" Sebastian complained. So here's a song about the monsoon season being a hassle.

Background:

   Despite not being aligned in any holiday at all, the song stayed in the holiday EP as a novelty track for the fun of it. Just like "Warm Cup," it's all for revisiting roots. Sebastian chose the blues as the style of the track as he felt it was fitting for his hatred with strong rains, especially with the nature of blues as a heartfelt speech about the singer's circumstances.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: December 1, 2024
  • First Released In: A Breeze of Hernandez
  • Genre/s: Holiday, Blues, Lo-fi
