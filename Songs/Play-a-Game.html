Song Bio:

   Relieving yourself from the unhappy "Again Someday", this song helps to get you back on your feet. With the first use of synths in a song by Sub, this only further expands his catalogue and songwriting skills.

Background:

   Despite the song originating around the same time as "Listen To This Song" and "Again Someday", the final material lacked resemblance of the demo apart from the minimalist chord progression. The idea of using synthesizers became a thing during the songwriting process when Sub took an interest in using it as a song's backbone.
