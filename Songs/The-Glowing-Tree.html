The Glowing Tree

Song Bio:

   As a Christmas special, this song brings the spirit of simplicity and comfort for the holidays. The song revolves selflessness and the happiness one gets in the Christmas season.

Background:

   The song was written with only one purpose at the time: a B-side for "(As Long As) You're Still Here". Eventually, people behind the scenes preferred the song to be the A-side of the single instead. This song introduced the woodwinds to Sub's discography. Its placement in the song was a substitute to a hum from the original composition.

Other Information:

  • Original Release Date: November 1, 2023
  • First Released In: "You're Still Here" b/w "(As Long As) You're Still Here" (2023)
  • Genre/s: Christmas, Folk
